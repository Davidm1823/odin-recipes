Sandwich with beef patty and cheese on top

Ingrediants

  • 600 g / 1.2 lb beef mince (ground beef) , at least 20% fat (Note 1 for quality)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Burgar Bun
  • Cheese

Recipe instructions

  • 1. Season the beef with salt pepper and oil.
  • 2. Make the beef into patties
  • 3. Cook on Medium heat until golden brown
  • 4. Add the cheese and put everything togther
